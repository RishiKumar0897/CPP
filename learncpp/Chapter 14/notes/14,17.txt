- member functions can be made constexpr 
- aggregates can be made constexpr 
- for class objects, we need to make the constructor constexpr as well  
    - if we want class to be able to be evaluated at compile-time, make member functions and constructor constexpr 


- constexpr members may be needed with non-constexpr/non-const objects 
- constexpr member functions may be const or non-const
